Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

After being hit by a radioactive web, Miles Morales, a young black man from Brooklyn, becomes Spider-Man, inspired by the legacy of the late Peter Parker. However, when he visits his idol's grave on a rainy night, he is surprised by the presence of Peter himself, wearing the hero's costume under an overcoat. The surprise is even greater when Miles discovers that he came from a parallel dimension, just like other versions of Spider-Man.
